Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to pay for your services?

No! Our services are completely free to you. We're compensated by insurance carriers when you enroll in a policy, so you never pay us directly.

How do you find the best rates?

We work with multiple insurance carriers and use our industry connections to compare rates and coverage. Our experience helps us know which carriers offer the best value for your specific situation.

Can you help if I already have insurance?

Absolutely! We can review your current coverage and often find better rates or more comprehensive coverage. Many clients save money by switching during open enrollment periods.

What areas do you serve?

While we're based in ST PETERSBURG, FL, we're licensed to help clients across multiple states. Contact us to confirm coverage in your area.

How long does the process take?

We can often provide quotes within minutes. The full enrollment process typically takes 15-30 minutes, and coverage can begin as soon as the next day for some policies.
